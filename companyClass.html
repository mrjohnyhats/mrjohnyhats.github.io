AGREEER INTERFACE

The Agreeer interface models something that would agree to a business agreement. A clas that implements Agreeer must somehow store business agreements, remove busines agreements, and check if its participating in a certain business agreement

COMPANY CLASS

The company class implements Agreeer and stores business agreements in an arraylist. It also stores products in an array list and can fix the price of a certain product to a fixed price specified by a trust in its list of business agreements. It also has methods to join and get out of business agreements (like trusts).
