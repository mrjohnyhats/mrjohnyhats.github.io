My project was to create an object that represents a trust or cartell between compaies. A trust is a group of companies that produce the same thing and agree to keep the prices of the products they have in common the same to prevent competition. For example, in a steel trust, companies would all agree to sell steel for $100/ton so that 1 company doesn't sell steel for $50/ton and force the other companies to sell steel for cheaper or lose popularity. Thanks to left wing politicans like Theodore Roosevelt and bills like the Sherman Act, the Clayton Act and the Federal Trade Commission Act, most of the trusts formed during the gilded age have been broken up and trusts are now illegal.

My project is built out of these classes:

  • a Trust class that inherets intance variables and methods from a BusinessAgreement abstract class
  • a Company class that implements an Agreeer interface that declares methods for something that agrees with a business agreement
  • a Product class that represents the product of a company. Companies have lists of products and Trusts have 1 product that represent the product that the members of the trust agree to fix
  • a TrustDriver class that makes a trust and random companies for the trust. The user gets to select properties of the trust
