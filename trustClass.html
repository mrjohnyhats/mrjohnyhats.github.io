BUSINESS AGREEMENT ABSTACT CLASS

The BusinessAgreement class represents a busines agreement. A business agreement has a name and a list of members (companies). It can show and modify its list of members and check if a company is a member.

TRUST CLASS

The Trust class extends the BusinessAgreement class. It contains its own fixedPriceProd which is a Product that represents the product that the trust is fixing and the price companies will fix the product to. The class contains methods for ordering member companies to fix their prices and methods to ban companies who make their prices lower than the trust's fixed price. It also has various utility methods.
