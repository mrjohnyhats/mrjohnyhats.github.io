ABOUT ME

Jonathan is good at bike riding and coding. He once agreed to ride his bike for 200km with his friend to San Francisco, up to Woodside and then circling foster city a couple times. It was very difficult but his love of biking made it worth the effort. Jonathan knows a lot about coding and likes to make his own programs. He enjoys the creative possibilities and control coding gives him. His experience programming from a young age makes him used to the challenge of writing readable code for big projects. Jonathan also likes to takes risks when he goes on biking adventures with his friends. He's used to riding in traffic on the margins of the road where uncaring and annoyed Bay Area drivers could hit him. He also likes to tinker with his bike and other things. He can also be a really hard worker if he's interested in what he's doing. Growing up with divorced immigrant parents, Jonathan has learned to be self reliant. He's been able to find a job at TheCoderSchool where he's able to teach kids coding of all ages in 1:1 sessions with no curriculum. He also helps lead coding summer camps at the same company where he is responsible for 10-15 elementary/middle school aged kids and is on the hook to show parents that their kids had fun and wrote a program while at camp. Jonathan aspires to be a programmer and hopes that his job will help him look good to employers.
